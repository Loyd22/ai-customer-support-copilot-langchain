# Customer Support FAQ

- **Document Title:** Customer Support FAQ
- **Document Type:** Customer-Facing Help Center
- **Intended Audience:** Customers
- **Last Updated:** March 12, 2026

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. How long does shipping take?
Most orders are processed within **1 to 2 business days**. Standard shipping usually takes **3 to 5 business days after dispatch**, and express shipping usually takes **1 to 2 business days after dispatch**.

### 2. How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, we send a tracking email. Tracking updates may take up to **24 hours** to appear after the label is created.

### 3. Can I cancel my order?
You may request cancellation while the order is still **Pending Payment**, **Confirmed**, or **Processing**. Once it reaches **Packed** or **Shipped**, cancellation is usually no longer possible.

### 4. What is your return window?
Eligible returns must be requested within **30 calendar days of delivery**.

### 5. What should I do if my item arrives damaged?
Please contact support within **7 calendar days of delivery** and include clear photos of the item, packaging, and shipping label.

### 6. What if I received the wrong item?
Report it within **7 calendar days of delivery**. We may offer a replacement or refund after review.

### 7. Do I have to pay for return shipping?
For change-of-mind returns, yes. If the item is damaged, defective, incorrect, or missing due to our error, we cover return shipping.

### 8. When will I receive my refund?
After an approved return is received and inspected, refunds are usually initiated within **3 business days**. Most payment providers post them within **5 to 10 business days**.

### 9. Are shipping fees refundable?
Original shipping fees are usually refunded only when the return is due to a company error, damaged item, defective item, or wrong item sent.

### 10. What items cannot be returned?
Final sale items, gift cards, personalized items, and hygiene-sensitive items once opened are generally non-returnable.

### 11. What happens if my package is delayed?
Carrier delays can happen. If your tracking is still updating, please continue monitoring it. Support may open a review if the shipment exceeds the normal delay threshold.

### 12. When is a package considered lost?
A package may be treated as lost if tracking shows no movement for **7 calendar days after the latest estimated delivery date**, or if the carrier confirms it is lost.

### 13. My tracking says delivered, but I do not have the package. What should I do?
Please check with household members, neighbors, reception, or building staff, and allow up to **48 hours** for possible carrier scan correction.

### 14. Can I change my shipping address after ordering?
Address changes are not guaranteed. Once an order reaches **Packed** or **Shipped**, changes are usually no longer possible.

### 15. What if my refund is less than expected?
A partial refund may be applied if the returned item is missing parts, shows unexpected use, or does not match the approved return condition.

### 16. Do you offer replacements?
Yes. Replacements may be offered for damaged, defective, incorrect, or missing items caused by fulfillment error.

### 17. What if I want to speak to a manager?
Our support team will review your case first. If manager review is required, the request can be escalated.

### 18. What order statuses will I see?
Common statuses include **Pending Payment**, **Confirmed**, **Processing**, **Packed**, **Shipped**, **Out for Delivery**, **Delivered**, **Cancelled**, **Returned**, and **Refunded**.
