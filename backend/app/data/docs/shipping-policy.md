# Shipping Policy

- **Document Title:** Shipping Policy
- **Document Type:** Customer-Facing Policy
- **Intended Audience:** Customers
- **Last Updated:** March 12, 2026

## Overview

Northbeam Goods ships physical orders placed through our online store. Delivery times depend on the shipping method selected at checkout, item availability, payment approval, and carrier performance.

## Order Processing Time

Most in-stock orders are processed within **1 to 2 business days** after payment is successfully authorized.

Orders may take longer to process when:

- Payment requires manual review
- The order contains high-demand items
- The shipping address needs verification
- Severe weather, service interruptions, or peak volume affect operations

## Shipping Methods

### Standard Shipping
- Estimated delivery: **3 to 5 business days after dispatch**

### Express Shipping
- Estimated delivery: **1 to 2 business days after dispatch**

Delivery timelines begin after the order leaves our facility, not at the moment the order is placed.

## Order Statuses

Customers may see the following order statuses:

- **Pending Payment**: payment has not cleared yet
- **Confirmed**: order has been accepted
- **Processing**: order is being prepared
- **Packed**: order is packed and awaiting carrier pickup
- **Shipped**: package has left our facility
- **Out for Delivery**: carrier is attempting delivery
- **Delivered**: carrier marked the package delivered
- **Cancelled**: order was canceled before shipment
- **Returned**: returned item is in transit or received back
- **Refunded**: refund has been processed

## Tracking

Tracking information is sent by email once the order is marked **Shipped**. Customers should allow up to **24 hours** for tracking updates to appear after the label is created.

## Delivery Delays

Estimated delivery windows are not guaranteed unless explicitly sold as a guaranteed service. Delays may happen because of:

- carrier backlogs
- weather events
- delivery restrictions
- incorrect or incomplete address details
- holiday or peak-season volume

If a shipment is delayed, support may advise the customer to wait a reasonable carrier update window before a claim is opened.

## Failed Delivery

A delivery may fail when:

- the address is incomplete or invalid
- the carrier cannot access the location
- the recipient is unavailable when signature is required
- the package is refused

If a package is returned to Northbeam Goods due to customer address error or refusal:

- reshipment fees may apply
- original shipping charges are generally not refunded
- the order may be refunded minus return shipping or carrier fees where applicable

## Lost Package Handling

A package may be treated as lost when:

- it shows **no tracking movement for 7 calendar days after the latest estimated delivery date**, or
- the carrier confirms the package is lost

When a package qualifies as lost, Northbeam Goods may offer:

- a replacement, or
- a refund to the original payment method

Customers may be asked to confirm:

- shipping address
- delivery notes
- whether neighbors or building staff accepted the package

## Delivered but Not Received

If tracking shows **Delivered** but the customer cannot locate the package, support may ask the customer to:

- check with household members, neighbors, reception, or building staff
- verify the shipping address
- wait up to **48 hours** for possible final carrier scan correction

If the package still cannot be located, support will review the case based on delivery evidence, order history, and carrier information.

## Address Changes

Address changes are not guaranteed after an order is placed. Once an order reaches **Packed** or **Shipped** status, changes are usually no longer possible.

## Shipping Restrictions

Some items may not be eligible for express shipping because of size, handling needs, or carrier limitations. Availability is shown at checkout where possible.
