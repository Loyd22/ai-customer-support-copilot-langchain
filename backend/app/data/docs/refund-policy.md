# Refund Policy

- **Document Title:** Refund Policy
- **Document Type:** Customer-Facing Policy
- **Intended Audience:** Customers
- **Last Updated:** March 12, 2026

## Overview

At **Northbeam Goods**, we offer refunds for eligible orders when items are returned within the approved return window and meet the conditions below. Refunds are issued to the original payment method only.

This policy applies to physical products purchased directly from Northbeam Goods through our online store.

## Refund Eligibility

A refund may be approved when all of the following are true:

- The refund request is submitted within **30 calendar days of delivery**
- The item is unused or gently handled for inspection only
- The item is returned with original parts, accessories, and packaging where reasonably possible
- The item is not listed as non-returnable or final sale

Refunds may also be approved for:

- Items that arrive **damaged**
- Items that arrive **defective**
- Items that are **incorrect**
- Orders confirmed as **lost in transit**
- Cancellations approved before shipment

## Non-Eligible Refunds

Refunds are generally not available for:

- Final sale items
- Gift cards
- Personalized or custom-made items
- Hygiene-sensitive items once opened
- Items damaged after delivery due to misuse, improper installation, or normal wear
- Returns requested after the 30-day return window
- Items returned without major components or intentionally altered from original condition

## Refund Request Windows

### Standard Returns
- Request must be submitted within **30 calendar days of delivery**

### Damaged, Defective, or Incorrect Items
- Report the issue within **7 calendar days of delivery**
- Clear photos may be required before approval

### Lost Packages
- A package may be treated as lost if:
  - tracking shows no movement for **7 calendar days after the latest estimated delivery date**, or
  - the carrier confirms the package is lost

## Refund Amounts

### Full Refund
A full refund is typically issued when:

- A return is approved and the item is received in eligible condition
- The order was canceled before shipment
- The wrong item was sent and returned
- The item arrived damaged or defective and Northbeam Goods approves a refund instead of a replacement
- A package is confirmed lost in transit

### Partial Refund
A partial refund may be issued when:

- The returned item shows signs of use beyond inspection
- Accessories, manuals, or parts are missing
- The item is returned in damaged condition not caused by shipping
- The return is accepted as a one-time exception outside normal condition standards

When a partial refund is applied, support will explain the reason clearly in the case record and in the customer response.

## Shipping Charges

### Original Shipping Fees
- Original shipping charges are refunded only when:
  - the item was damaged, defective, incorrect, or
  - Northbeam Goods made a fulfillment error

### Return Shipping Fees
- For change-of-mind returns, the customer is responsible for return shipping
- For damaged, defective, incorrect, or company-error returns, Northbeam Goods covers return shipping or provides a return label

## Refund Processing Timeline

Once an eligible return is received and inspected:

- Refund approval or rejection is issued within **3 business days**
- Approved refunds are sent to the original payment method
- Most banks and payment providers post the refund within **5 to 10 business days**

For canceled orders that never shipped:

- Refunds are usually initiated within **3 business days** of cancellation approval

## Exceptions

Northbeam Goods may deny a refund when:

- The item does not match the approved return request
- The customer returns a different item
- Evidence suggests misuse, tampering, or fraud
- The request conflicts with a prior approved replacement resolution

## Escalation Triggers

A refund case should be reviewed by a senior support specialist or manager when:

- The customer disputes a partial refund
- The refund amount is over **$500**
- The customer reports multiple unresolved refund issues on the same order
- There is a fraud concern, chargeback threat, or payment dispute
- The customer requests a manager after policy has been explained
- A package shows conflicting delivery evidence

## Short Examples

- **Approved:** “My order arrived damaged and I reported it two days after delivery.”  
  Eligible for review. Photos may be required.

- **Approved:** “I changed my mind and requested a return 10 days after delivery.”  
  Eligible if the item is returnable and still in acceptable condition.

- **Not Approved:** “I used the product for six weeks and now want a refund because I no longer need it.”  
  Not eligible if outside the return window or used beyond inspection.
