# Returns and Replacements Policy

- **Document Title:** Returns and Replacements Policy
- **Document Type:** Customer-Facing Policy
- **Intended Audience:** Customers
- **Last Updated:** March 12, 2026

## Overview

Northbeam Goods accepts eligible returns and replacements for physical products purchased directly from our store. This policy covers change-of-mind returns, damaged items, defective items, and incorrect shipments.

## Return Eligibility

An item may be returned when all of the following apply:

- The return request is submitted within **30 calendar days of delivery**
- The item is in original or near-original condition
- The item includes required components and accessories
- The item is not listed as non-returnable

Customers should not send an item back before return approval is issued.

## Replacement Eligibility

A replacement may be offered instead of a refund when:

- the item arrived damaged
- the item arrived defective
- the wrong item was shipped
- a required component was missing due to packing error

Replacement requests for damaged, defective, or incorrect items must be reported within **7 calendar days of delivery**.

## Proof Requirements

Northbeam Goods may require proof before approving a return or replacement, including:

- clear photos of the item
- photos of the packaging and shipping label
- description of the issue
- order number
- photo or video of the defect, where relevant

Failure to provide reasonable proof may delay or prevent approval.

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items are generally not eligible for return or replacement unless they arrived damaged or incorrect:

- final sale items
- gift cards
- personalized or custom-made items
- hygiene-sensitive items once opened
- items with missing serial labels or intentionally removed identifiers

## Return Shipping Responsibility

### Customer-Paid Return Shipping
The customer is responsible for return shipping when the return is due to:

- change of mind
- ordering the wrong item
- preference issues not caused by product defect

### Company-Paid Return Shipping
Northbeam Goods covers return shipping when the issue is due to:

- damage in transit
- defect
- wrong item sent
- missing item or missing part caused by fulfillment error

## Return Review and Inspection

Once a return arrives:

- it is inspected within **3 business days**
- support confirms whether the return matches the approved request
- the final outcome is recorded as refund, replacement, or rejection

## Replacement Process

If a replacement is approved:

- the original item may need to be returned first, depending on issue type and item value
- once approved, the replacement order is prepared within **1 to 2 business days**
- the replacement is shipped using standard shipping unless otherwise approved

## Exceptions and Denials

A return or replacement may be denied if:

- the request is outside the allowed time window
- the issue reported does not match the item received
- the item shows misuse, alteration, or non-manufacturing damage
- the customer sends back a different item
- the return was never approved

## Short Examples

- **Return example:** “I received the item 12 days ago and want to return it because it does not fit my space.”  
  Usually eligible if the item is returnable and still in acceptable condition.

- **Replacement example:** “The wrong color and model were sent, and I reported it the day after delivery.”  
  Usually eligible for replacement or refund review.

- **Not eligible:** “I opened a hygiene-sealed item and changed my mind.”  
  Not returnable unless it arrived damaged or incorrect.
